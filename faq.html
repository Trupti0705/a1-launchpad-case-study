❓ Frequently Asked Questions

⏱️ How long does a typical repair take?
Most repairs are completed within 2–3 business days. However, some issues may take longer depending on part availability and product type.
🚚 Do you offer pickup and delivery services?
Yes! We provide doorstep pickup and delivery for devices in select areas. You’ll be notified during the request process if this option is available to you.
🛠️ What kinds of devices can I send for repair?
We currently support smartphones, laptops, tablets, and select home electronics. You can check supported devices during request submission.
💳 What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, and online wallets. Payment is collected only after the repair is complete and approved by you.
🔐 Is my data safe during repairs?
Absolutely. We follow strict data protection guidelines. However, we strongly recommend backing up your data before sending in your device.
